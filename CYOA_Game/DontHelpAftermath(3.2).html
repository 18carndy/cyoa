You brush past a low-hanging treebranch and some underbrush before breaking through into a small clearing. The other vikings have already found places to sit and take a breath. Your captain, Ragnarr, is wringing out his damp clothes.

You find a dry stone to sit on and take off your boots, now completely wet. As you pour out what seems like a bucket of water from them, you look up to see Haraldr break through the treeline.

He tiredly moves towards where you are sitting and takes a seat on a stump beside you.

"You alright?"
"Took you long enough."
"You look like shit."
