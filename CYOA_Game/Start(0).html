The Great Raid

"Steel yourselves!" your captain bellows over the roaring sound of the crashing waves on wood.

You are a viking on your first raid on foreign shores. At the ripe age of 16 years old, you were expected to join your shield-brothers to gain glory and riches in the lands of the British heathens. Currently, you're standing amongst your brothers-in-arms on a gokstad (a viking longboat) huddled together with your hands on oars and bracing yourselves against the elements.

Another wave crashes against the stern and the wooden boat threatens to split in two under the impact.

"I see land!" shouts Haraldr from beside you. You lean over the side of the ship and in the distance you can make a out a faint mass.

"To the oars men!" yells the captain.

You grab your oar and start rowing like your life depended on it.
