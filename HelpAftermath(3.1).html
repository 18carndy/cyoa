You brush past a low-hanging treebranch and some underbrush before breaking through into a small clearing. The other vikings have already found places to sit and take a breath. Your captain, Ragnarr, is wringing out his damp clothes.

"Thank you." You feel a hand on your shoulder and see a tired but grateful look on Haraldr's face.

"No problem."
"By Odin, learn how to fasten a stake!"
I gruff and shrug his hand off my shoulder.
